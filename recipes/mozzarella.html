Mozzarella

Description

Mozarella sticks are typically coated in breadcrumbs and deep-fried to create a crispy outer layer while keeping the cheese inside soft and gooey

Ingridients

  • Mozzarella
  • all purpose flour
  • 2 Large eggs
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. Prepare the cheese
  2. Set up a breading station
  3. Bread the mozzarella sticks
  4. Freeze the breaded mozzarella sticks
  5. Heat the oil for frying
  6. Carefully add a few frozen mozzarella sticks to the hot oil, making sure not to overcrowd the pan.
  7. Serve and enjoy while Hot
